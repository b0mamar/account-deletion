Account Data Deletion Request

At Time4Test, we respect your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so by following the instructions below.

📱 Method 1: Delete via the App (Fastest)

  1. Open the Time4Test app.
  2. Go to the Profile tab in the bottom navigation.
  3. Scroll down and tap on Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your decision. All your data will be permanently wiped immediately.

✉️ Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to the app or your device, you can request manual data deletion. Please send an email from the address associated with your account.
